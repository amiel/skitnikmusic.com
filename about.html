Skitnik is a Bellingham, WA based ensemble.

We call ourselves a Balkan band, but really we just play whatever music interests us. The band consists of: clarinet, alto sax, bari sax, percussion, trumpet, concertina, mandolin and accordion. Sometimes we play other instruments, like bass clarinet, oboe, and saw.
